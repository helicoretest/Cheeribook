Welcome to Cheeribook! We are Tutu and Marina, the faces behind the photobook creation service that aims to capture the beauty of life's special moments.

Connecting with people is a vital aspect of our work at Cheeribook. Tutu loves communicating through her art and seeing her work bring joy to others. Marina values getting to know people and making them feel seen and valued. Her philosophy is that people will forget what you said or did but never forget how you made them feel.
At Cheeribook, we value being community-oriented and strive to capture the memories and value of a community by learning about it and creating a free souvenir to share with others. We're also family-oriented and promote work flexibility and accommodation for family needs. Our goal is to create a work environment allowing our team to choose their office hours and better fit the team's schedule.

How Does We Work

01.

Upload photo to a secure share folder

02.

Our book artist will go through your photos and check the photo quality for each one

03.

The book artist then arranges photos and photo touches your photos. It can be adjusting the lighting to edit an object out or edit some details. This is a very important step so that we can optimize your photo quality.

04.

We will then send out a first draft for you to approve. If everything looks good to you, then we will finish up the photobook and send out for a final approval.

05.

You will receive a shareable and printable file. Yes! It’s completely yours so you can print as many copies as you like.

06.

The book can be printed anywhere, but if you would like us to make the order for you, please note we will charge a $9.99.
